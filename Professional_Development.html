Professional Development

Creating engaging, impactful learning experiences for educators

Professional Growth Framework

My approach to professional development is built on the understanding that effective teacher learning should be responsive, differentiated, and directly connected to classroom practice. I design experiences that transform teacher practice and measurably improve student outcomes.

Tiered coaching models

I implement differentiated coaching approaches that meet teachers where they are in their development, providing the right level of support at the right time.

  • Targeted observation and feedback cycles
  • Differentiated support based on teacher experience and needs
  • Data-driven coaching priorities

Adult learning principles

My professional development design honors how adults learn best, ensuring that learning experiences are relevant, practical, and immediately applicable.

  • Problem-based learning with real classroom scenarios
  • Collaborative learning structures
  • Reflection opportunities to deepen understanding

Teacher leadership development

I build sustainable improvement by identifying and developing teacher leaders who can amplify effective practices throughout the school community.

  • Mentorship pathways for emerging leaders
  • Facilitation skills development
  • Collaborative planning and co-teaching models

Impact & Results

My tiered coaching model and leadership development program resulted in a 9% increase in teacher retention (from 73% to 82%) and 26% of staff being promoted to leadership roles over a three-year period.
