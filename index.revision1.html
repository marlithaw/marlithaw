My Journey

I lead with a systems-focused approach—creating learning ecosystems that cultivate growth, strengthen capacity, and transform lived experiences into sustainable leadership. Since beginning my journey as a Teach For America corps member in 2013, I've evolved my practice into a lasting commitment to fostering growth and impact that extend far beyond the classroom.

Over the past year, I stepped away from campus leadership to step into caregiving for my grandmother—a role that reinforced my commitment to sustainable systems, deepened my understanding of intergenerational healing, and strengthened my resolve to lead with integrity, even in uncertain terrain.

Philosophy & Approach

Systems Over Personalities

I believe that effective educational leadership is about creating robust systems that transcend individual personalities. When instructional frameworks are built with intention and clarity, they produce consistent results regardless of staffing challenges.

Capacity Building

My approach centers on building capacity in others through personalized coaching and targeted support. By honoring individuals' unique strengths while providing clear structures, I develop teams that exceed the sum of their parts.

Beyond the Classroom

My background spans education and law, bringing a unique perspective to educational leadership. This interdisciplinary experience informs my approach to systems design, equity, and sustainable impact.

Literacy Excellence

My 5th Grade 3-Year STAAR Outcomes

Consistent Excellence:

For three consecutive years (2022-2024), my 5th grade students consistently outperformed district and state benchmarks. Most impressive was the 2024 achievement without a dedicated teacher, where students exceeded state averages by +16% in writing process skills.

Achievement Highlights

Writing Process Skills +16% vs State Average
Editing & Punctuation +11% vs State Average
Reading Comprehension +4% vs State Average

These results are especially impressive considering that our 2024 cohort had no dedicated reading teacher, demonstrating the power of sustainable instructional systems.
