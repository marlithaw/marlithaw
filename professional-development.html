The Challenge

The Challenge: A Developing Literacy Team (2022-2024)

IDEA Brackenridge Academy navigated significant staffing headwinds, with 83% of the teaching team possessing less than two years of experience. Adding to this, 72% of educators were uncertified and lacked formal pedagogical training.

Rising to the Occasion: Unwavering Dedication Amidst High Expectations

Despite these considerable staffing limitations, teachers demonstrated remarkable diligence in ensuring continuous student learning and growth.

The Solution: Empowerment Through Data and Collaboration

Data-informed interventions, combined with collaborative coaching, became key strategies to empower teachers. This approach enabled educators to effectively address learning gaps while simultaneously fostering student agency.

Implementation

Tiered Coaching Framework--Guidepost for Excellent Teaching (GET)

Tier 1: Foundation Building (All Teachers)

  • Weekly grade-level team planning with scripted lesson guidance
  • Biweekly classroom walkthroughs with immediate verbal feedback & Teachboost documentation to promote growth
  • Monthly professional development focused on core teaching competencies
  • Weekly data deep-dive to inform instructional pacing

Tier 2: Targeted Development (Novice/Developing Teachers)

  • Twice-weekly co-teaching or model lessons
  • Daily quick checks with "bite-sized" action steps
  • Video analysis with guided self-reflection
  • Skill-focused learning walks with peer teachers

Tier 3: Advanced Leadership (High-Performing Teachers)

  • Mentorship responsibilities with recognition systems
  • Action research opportunities targeting campus needs
  • Leadership roles in professional development delivery
  • Collaborative curriculum adaptation projects
