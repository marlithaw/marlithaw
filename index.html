About Me

I am an experienced educational leader, instructional coach, and professional learning facilitator with expertise in literacy and math instruction, data-driven intervention, and curriculum implementation. My passion lies in designing and leading engaging professional development that improves teacher effectiveness and student outcomes.

With a background in both education and law, I bring a unique perspective to educational leadership, focusing on systems that drive equity and close learning gaps. My work has consistently yielded measurable improvements in teacher growth and retention, even amidst challenging conditions.

I believe that effective schools are built on robust systems, not just individual charisma. Throughout my career, I've championed the philosophy that teacher development should be layered, responsive, anchored in clear routines, and grounded in adult learning theory.
