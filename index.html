About Me

With a background in law and education, I bring an analytical, equity-driven lens to literacy leadership. My work focuses on building durable, scalable systems that empower even novice or substitute teachers to deliver rigorous, meaningful instruction. I’ve led campus-wide gains in reading while navigating teacher shortages, trauma, and instructional instability.
